All these whole festival days, people go round the Mandats with their bicycles, motorbikes or open-top cars. Everyone are throwing water at each other except the monks, nuns, elderly or sick people and pregnant women to show them some respect. Most of the shops and restaurants are closed during these days but there are many places throughout the whole country which offering the free food-donations which is called “Satuditha” and anyone can go and join.In Myanmar,the prices of foodstuffs and offerings go up around Thingyan as many novitiation and ordination ceremonies are held then.
