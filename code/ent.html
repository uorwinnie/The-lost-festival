The band started in the 1920s in the former royal capital with a group of amateur musicians. It rose to fame partly due to its leader Myoma Nyein, one of the most distinguished musicians and composers in Myanmar's history, as well as its patriotic songs during colonial rule. It became synonymous with Thingyan, Myanmar's traditional water festival, as the band had composed a good number of golden oldies which they perform during Thingyan in Mandalay. Every year during Thingyan, the band tours the city in their vehicle with members playing music and dancing on the vehicle. The goose is the signature of Myoma.
Dancing the traditional thingyan dance is necessary in Thingyan festival including pandal opening and closing ceremonies. Over 800 people perform in opening ceremony. Many yein dance group perform beautifully at pandals during Thingyan festival.
Yein is a form of group dance that features multiple dancers synchronously dancing and moving their heads, waists, feet, and hands to the beats of music, often performed by a traditional Burmese orchestra called hsaing waing or drums. It is commonly performed by troupes during Thingyan.
There are a lot of movies with thingyan background. The OST songs of thingyan movies, especially old movies are really famous. Television channels use to transmit old thingyan movies during thingyan festival.Among thingyan movies, "Thingyan Moe"(meaning- rain that rains during thingyan period) is all time most popular movie. New casts of the movies also become famous and the olds become more famous along with the movie.
