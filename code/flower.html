As a symbolization of strength and durability, this kind of tree can be used for furniture design and making. The golden fragrant flowers of Padauk are also considered as the national flower of Myanmar. While the tree symbolizes strength, the flower represents youth, love and romance with its fragrant scent.

Blooming during April, the flower seems to be a sign of the first showers in April and the New Year celebrations as well.Burmese women wear Padauk on their head and also offer before our household shrines. If no thingyan shower for the month of april, we rarely see the beautiful Padauk on Thingyan month.

Burmese say that padauk is a sign of faith. In burmese poems, they say padauk blooms once in the month of a year, during Thingyan.That's why people call padauk as the signature of Thingyan. Youths of Myanmar use to compare their love with padauk.
