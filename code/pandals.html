Thingyan festival, water festival, is held in different styles in different places. Mandalay Thingyan and Yangon Thingyan are most crowded places as they are big cities of Myanmar.
Walking thingyan in Mandalay,floating water festival in Inlay lake, Rakhine traditional thingyan where there water is placed in Rakhine traditional long boat(long-hlay).
Years ago, people didn't use water pipes for water splashing . They used sprinkling of scented water in a silver bowl and just touch to the others gentally using sprigs of thabyay (Jambul) Everybody from various living classes participates this festival.
